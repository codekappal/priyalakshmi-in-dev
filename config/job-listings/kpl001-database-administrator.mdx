We are looking for a **Database Administrator** to join our team at Kappal Software. You will be responsible for managing, optimizing and securing our databases to ensure reliability and high performance.
<br/><br/>

### **Responsibilities**
- Design, implement and maintain high-availability databases.
- Optimize database performance and troubleshoot slow queries.
- Ensure data integrity, security and compliance.
- Develop backup and recovery strategies to prevent data loss.
- Collaborate with developers to improve database efficiency.
<br/><br/>

### **Requirements**
- Strong expertise in **PostgreSQL, MySQL and NoSQL databases**.
- Experience in **database performance tuning and query optimization**.
- Knowledge of **database security best practices**.
- Familiarity with **cloud-based database solutions (AWS RDS, Azure, GCP)**.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and teamwork.
